======================

Turn competitor names into a full intelligence report — SWOT, positioning map, feature matrix, pricing analysis, and strategic recommendations.

- Product managers
- Strategists
- Founders
- Consultants
- Market analysts

## Input

- List of competitors or a market category
- Optional: specific dimensions to compare

## Output

- Build a competitive positioning report before a product launch
- Identify white-space opportunities in a crowded market
